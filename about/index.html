The Bell Path Group Story

Built on values, from the ground up

Chris Lau is the Managing Partner of Bell Path Group. After more than 10 years of advising and investing in lower middle market businesses, he is now investing on behalf of himself and a select group of family and friends.

Chris believes in investing and improving businesses the right way, treating business owners, customers, employees, and suppliers with the respect and transparency they deserve. Bell Path Group exists because he wanted to build something that reflected those values from the ground up.
