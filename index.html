Overview

We invest with a long-term horizon, preserving what you've built.

We acquire a majority or minority ownership position and offer full flexibility on post-closing operational involvement. Whether you want to step away or stay involved, we honor that choice.

Our goal is not a quick flip. We invest with a long-term horizon, preserving what you've built while applying the resources and expertise needed to help it grow.

We do not have institutional outside investors with ulterior motives or short time horizons. Our family ownership allows full autonomy on transaction structuring, industry exposure, and transitions.

We have successfully closed on acquisitions through Bell Path Group and in prior work experiences. We understand that no business is perfect; we can (and have) worked through messy financials and material business issues to get transactions done. We can proudly provide references to both sellers and employees from businesses we have worked with in the past.
